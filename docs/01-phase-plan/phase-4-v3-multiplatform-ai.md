# Phase 4: V3 Multi-Platform and AI

## Objective
Expand distribution channels and improve post selection quality with AI-assisted ranking.

## Scope (Delta from V2)
- Additional publishers: Instagram, Twitter/X, WhatsApp Channel, Facebook
- AI scoring/ranking of candidate products
- Click tracking and short URL redirects
- Async event pipeline and analytics storage

## Non-Goals
- Billing and agency mode
- Full white-label features

## Exit Criteria
- At least two additional publishers are production-ready
- Click events are captured with source platform
- AI picker can be toggled per user or plan
