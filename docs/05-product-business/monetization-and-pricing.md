# Monetization and Pricing

## Pricing Model
Monthly subscriptions with feature gating.

## Plans
- FREE: limited daily posts and basic features
- PRO: higher volume, schedule control, expanded integrations
- AGENCY: multi-account and advanced capabilities

## Revenue Logic
- User earns affiliate commissions externally.
- Platform captures recurring SaaS subscription revenue.

## Pricing Validation Approach
- Start with planned pricing from source doc
- Review activation, retention, and conversion after initial launch
- Adjust packaging before V4 scale expansion
